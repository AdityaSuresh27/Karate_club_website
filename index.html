Karate Styles

There are many styles of karate, including Shotokan, Goju-Ryu and Wado-Ryu.

Shotokan: Known for its powerful linear techniques and deep stances. Effective in developing speed and power.

Goju-Ryu: Combines hard and soft techniques, focusing on close-quarter combat and breathing control. Excellent for self-defense and flexibility.

Wado-Ryu: Blends karate with jujitsu, emphasizing body shifting to avoid attacks. Great for enhancing agility and evasiveness.
